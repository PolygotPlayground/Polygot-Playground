🇰🇪 🇹🇿 Swahili Greetings

Here are common Swahili greetings and their meanings:

  • Habari – Hello (General greeting)
  • Jambo – Hello (Casual)
  • Shikamoo – Greeting for elders (Formal)
  • Marahaba – Response to Shikamoo
  • Hujambo – How are you? (Singular)
  • Hamjambo – How are you? (Plural)
  • Nzuri – I’m fine
  • Asante – Thank you
  • Karibu – You’re welcome
