🇰🇪 🇹🇿 Swahili Greetings

Here are common Swahili greetings and their meanings:

  • Habari - Hello (formal + informal)
  • sawa - okay (Response to Habari)
  • Habari za asubhi - Good morning
  • Habari za mchana - Good afternoon
  • Habari za jioni - Good evening
  • Lala salama - Good night
  • Hali yako vipi? - How are you? (formal)
  • Mambo? - What's up?
  • Mambo - Hey! (Informal)
  • Umekuwaje? / Uko vipi? - How are you? (Informal)
  • Nzuri - I'm fine
  • Shikamoo - Hello (formal)
  • Marahaba - Welcome (Response to Shikamoo)
  • Poa - Cool / Good / Okay (Response to Mambo)
  • Salama - Goodbye / Farewell (peace / safety)
  • Hujambo - How are you? (Formal)
  • Sijambo - I'm fine (Response to Hujambo)
  • Karibu - Welcome
  • Kwa heri - Goodbye (kenyan)
  • Kwa herini - Goodbye (formal + informal)
  • Lala salama - Sleep well
  • Tuonane tena - See you soon
